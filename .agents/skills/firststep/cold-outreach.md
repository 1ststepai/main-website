# 1stStep Cold Outreach

Use when writing 1stStep.ai cold emails, DMs, LinkedIn messages, or follow-up sequences.

Checklist:
- Personalize by industry, role, current problem, or visible trigger.
- Keep message short.
- Use one concrete offer.
- CTA should be low-friction.
- Include follow-up sequence and stop condition.

Do not:
- Use hype, fake familiarity, fake scarcity, or spammy language.
- Promise guaranteed results.

Output:
- Sequence, variants, subject lines, personalization fields, follow-up timing.
