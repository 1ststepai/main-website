# Cold Outreach

## When To Use

Use this skill when writing cold emails, LinkedIn DMs, social posts, warm follow-ups, small business group posts, or outreach sequences for 1stStep.ai.

## Project-Specific Rules

Do not pitch generic web design. Lead with the free checker or a specific workflow observation.

Primary outreach CTA:

Run your idea through the free App Idea Checker.

Secondary CTA:

Want me to send a quick 3-point website/workflow audit?

Good audiences:

- founders
- small business owners
- contractors/home services
- consultants
- agencies
- service businesses
- operators with spreadsheet/text/manual workflow pain

## Copy/UX Rules

- Keep messages short.
- Avoid hype.
- Personalize by business type, workflow pain, or website gap.
- Make the ask small.
- Link to `/app-idea-viability-checker.html` when sharing the checker.

## Safety Constraints

- Do not guarantee results.
- Do not imply a personal relationship that does not exist.
- Do not use manipulative urgency.
- Do not scrape or add private data to the repo.
- Do not add email automation infrastructure unless explicitly requested.

## Acceptance Checklist

- Message is short enough to read on a phone.
- CTA is clear.
- The checker is framed as useful, not as a hard sales pitch.
- No guarantees or fake proof are included.
