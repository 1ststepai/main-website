# Offer Packaging

## When To Use

Use this skill when adding or revising service sections, pricing anchors, package cards, proposal-style copy, or offer pages.

## Project-Specific Rules

Use three simple offer buckets:

1. Website + Lead Capture System
   Starting at $1,500

2. Internal Tool or Dashboard
   Starting at $3,500

3. MVP App Build
   Starting at $5,000

Do not present 1stStep.ai as a generic dev shop. Frame each offer around a clear business problem and next step.

## Copy/UX Rules

- Explain who each offer is for.
- Explain what problem it solves.
- Explain what is included in the first version.
- Explain what should wait until later.
- Every offer CTA should route to `/app-idea-viability-checker.html` unless the user specifically asks for a call-first CTA.

## Safety Constraints

- Do not overpromise scope at a fixed price.
- Use "starting at" language for pricing anchors.
- Do not guarantee launch dates, revenue, rankings, or funding.
- Do not enable payments, invoices, GHL forwarding, or repo creation.

## Acceptance Checklist

- Offer cards are easy to compare.
- Each offer has a business outcome, not just a feature list.
- Pricing is framed as a starting point.
- CTA routes to the checker by default.
