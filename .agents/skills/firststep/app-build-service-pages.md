# 1stStep App Build Service Pages

Use when writing 1stStep.ai pages for app builds, MVPs, automations, dashboards, internal tools, or AI workflows.

Checklist:
- Identify buyer, problem, build scope, delivery process, timeline, and handoff.
- Show examples/case studies if real.
- Make CTA specific: request build review, scope an MVP, book planning call.
- Explain technical terms in plain language.

Do not:
- Overclaim speed, quality, or business outcomes.
- Use generic "transform your business with AI" copy.

Output:
- Page outline, copy, FAQ, CTA, metadata.
