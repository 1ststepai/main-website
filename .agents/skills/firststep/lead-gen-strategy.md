# 1stStep Lead Gen Strategy

Use when planning 1stStep.ai lead generation channels, offers, funnels, or outbound campaigns.

Checklist:
- Define ICP, trigger event, pain, offer, channel, CTA, follow-up.
- Prioritize channels by audience concentration, intent, cost, speed, and proof.
- Map landing page, lead magnet, outbound, and follow-up.

Do not:
- Recommend channels without a reason.
- Add paid tools/APIs without approval.

Output:
- Channel shortlist, offer angle, campaign plan, tracking plan.
