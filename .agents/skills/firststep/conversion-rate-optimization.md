# Conversion Rate Optimization

## When To Use

Use this skill when improving the homepage, service sections, CTA hierarchy, pricing/package anchors, proof sections, mobile CTA behavior, or the flow from visitor to checker to saved lead to booked call.

## Project-Specific Rules

- Optimize every page around one primary conversion action: send users to `/app-idea-viability-checker.html`.
- Primary CTA: `Check Your App Idea`.
- Secondary CTA: `Book a Build Strategy Call`.
- The strongest funnel is: homepage -> checker -> saved lead -> Resend admin notification -> booking CTA.
- Prefer clear offer sections, proof, pricing anchors, and friction reduction over generic agency copy.

## Copy/UX Rules

- Make the next step obvious above the fold.
- Explain what 1stStep.ai builds in plain language: apps, websites, dashboards, MVPs, and workflow automations.
- Use outcome-led sections: launch faster, capture more leads, reduce manual work, prove demand, replace spreadsheets.
- Keep the App Idea Checker visible on the homepage and in footer/section CTAs.
- Do not bury the booking link, but do not make it compete with the checker on first-touch traffic.

## Safety Constraints

- Do not promise guaranteed revenue, rankings, funding, or app success.
- Do not add fake testimonials, fake logos, or fake case studies.
- Do not introduce paid AI calls.
- Do not enable GHL forwarding or repo creation.
- Do not break `/app-idea-viability-checker.html` or `/api/app-idea-checker`.

## Acceptance Checklist

- Primary CTA routes to `/app-idea-viability-checker.html`.
- Secondary CTA uses the approved booking URL.
- Page copy is specific, practical, and founder/operator-friendly.
- Mobile CTAs are visible and usable.
- Visitor can understand what 1stStep.ai does in under 5 seconds.
