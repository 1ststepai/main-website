# Landing Page Copy

## When To Use

Use this skill when writing or revising homepage copy, service page copy, App Idea Checker copy, offer sections, hero sections, FAQs, or product/service positioning.

## Project-Specific Rules

Write for founders, small businesses, and operators who want a working app, website, dashboard, internal tool, or workflow automation.

Every page should answer:

1. What does 1stStep.ai build?
2. Who is it for?
3. What problem does it solve?
4. What should the visitor do next?

## Copy/UX Rules

- Avoid vague AI-consulting language like "AI-powered solutions for modern businesses."
- Use direct language like "Turn your app idea or messy business workflow into a working first version."
- Lead with business outcomes:
  - launch faster
  - capture more leads
  - reduce manual work
  - prove demand before overbuilding
  - replace spreadsheets and messy workflows
- Keep paragraphs short and scannable.
- Prefer practical confidence over hype.

## Safety Constraints

- Do not make guarantee claims.
- Do not claim expertise, clients, results, or certifications that are not present in the repo.
- Do not reposition the main site as a resume builder, Real Rank product, or local SEO product.
- Do not add paid AI APIs or behavior changes.

## Acceptance Checklist

- Copy names the audience and business problem clearly.
- Primary CTA is `Check Your App Idea`.
- Secondary CTA is `Book a Build Strategy Call`.
- The page routes naturally into the checker.
- No generic agency filler remains in the edited area.
