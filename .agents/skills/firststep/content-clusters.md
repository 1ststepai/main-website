# Content Clusters

## When To Use

Use this skill when planning or writing blog posts, SEO support pages, lead magnet support content, email content, or educational pages for 1stStep.ai.

## Project-Specific Rules

Every content page should support the lead magnet and route to:

`/app-idea-viability-checker.html`

Target content clusters:

App idea validation:

- Is my app idea worth building?
- How to choose MVP features
- MVP build checklist
- App idea validation checklist
- How much does it cost to build an MVP?

Business workflow tools:

- When to replace spreadsheets with a custom tool
- Internal dashboard examples for small businesses
- How to automate manual admin work
- Client portal ideas for service businesses

Website + lead systems:

- Why your website is not converting leads
- Website lead capture checklist
- Best quote form setup for service businesses

## Copy/UX Rules

- Write useful, practical content for founders and operators.
- Include specific next steps.
- Add a natural CTA to the checker.
- Avoid long generic intros.

## Safety Constraints

- Do not generate fake stats or fake case studies.
- Do not keyword stuff.
- Do not publish claims that require evidence unless evidence exists.
- Do not introduce Real Rank/local SEO product messaging.

## Acceptance Checklist

- Topic supports one of the approved clusters.
- CTA routes to the checker.
- Content answers a practical buyer question.
- No unsupported claims are introduced.
